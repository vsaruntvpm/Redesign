Centralized Salary & Service Oversight Platform

Enables authorized department heads to view and verify officer salary and payslip details

Provides structured access to promotion history, increments, and designation changes

This platform is designed exclusively for Heads of Department to provide centralized oversight of government officers’ salary, promotion, and service-related records. It ensures accurate monitoring of pay structures, allowances, deductions, and official service history through a secure and authorized system.

The system ensures transparency by maintaining complete service records, including designation changes, effective dates, and official promotion orders, supporting procedural accuracy and policy compliance.
